Getting started

How it works
  1. Work through the 6 areas of life below. For each item, set two sliders:
  2. Importance — in your ideal happy life, how much does this matter? (0 = don't need it · 10 = essential)
  3. Satisfaction — how happy are you with this right now? (0 = very dissatisfied · 10 = fully satisfied)
  4. Use the first number that pops into your head — not the number you think it should be.
  5. When you're done, hit See my results at the bottom for your wheel and personalized insights — then Download PDF Report to save or share it. Your progress saves automatically as you go, so you can close this and come back.
